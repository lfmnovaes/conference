INTERNATIONAL JAVASCRIPT CONFERENCE 2021

Every year in April, International JavaScript Conference London is the go-to event for JavaScript enthusiasts.
This year, too, we look forward to meeting you again!
Because we want to ensure that the lively exchange of knowledge and ideas so typical of iJS London continues to work well, we were one of the first in the conference industry to develop our online concept.
OUR ANSWER TO CORONA: ONLINE
We will be meeting this time online, where you could join us from your home or office. In order to be accessible to all participants, all DevOpsCon London tracks will be streamed live. Using professional video technology, all lectures and workshops will be broadcast live. And that’s not all: You can also get in touch with our speakers and expo partners, even if you won’t be there in person!
As you can see – thanks to the innovative program and the numerous networking opportunities, the upcoming iJS London will provide the ideal framework for your continuing education.
On behalf of the entire International JavaScript Conference team: Sebastian Meyen (Global Content Director, S&S Media Group)
